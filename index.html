Important Notices for Job Seekers
—Cronos is a job advertising platform only. We are not a recruitment agency and we do not act on behalf of any employer or candidate in the hiring process.
—All applications submitted through this platform are sent directly to the hiring company. Cronos does not receive, review, filter, manage or track any applications. Please direct all follow-up queries to the employer.
—No AI is used in the application process. Cronos does not use artificial intelligence to screen, rank, shortlist or assess any candidate. Your application goes to the employer exactly as submitted.
—Cronos is not responsible for the accuracy of job listings. While all employers are verified before posting, we do not guarantee the completeness or currency of any advertised vacancy.
—Cronos will never ask you for payment to apply for a job, access a vacancy, or obtain a reference. If any listing or person requests payment, please report it to us immediately.
—We strongly advise all candidates to verify the legitimacy of any employer before sharing personal information or attending interviews at unfamiliar premises.

Cronos Verification Required
To protect job seekers from scams and fraudulent listings, all employers must complete our verification process before any vacancy can go live on the platform.
Identity verification — We verify the identity of the hiring contact and their authority to recruit on behalf of the company.
Business registration check — Your company must be a registered legal entity (CIPC or equivalent). We verify this independently before approving your account.
Zero tolerance for fraud — Any fraudulent listing will be removed immediately and the employer account permanently banned. Legal action may be pursued where applicable.
Fast turnaround — Applications are reviewed within 1–2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with next steps once submitted.
