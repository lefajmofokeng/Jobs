Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Cronos Jobs is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Who We Are

Cronos Jobs is a South African online job advertising platform. References to "we", "us" or "Cronos Jobs" in this policy refer to the Cronos Jobs platform and its operators.

For privacy enquiries, contact us at support@cronos.co.za

2. Information We Collect

From job seekers:

  • Name, email address, phone number and location
  • CV and supporting documents uploaded during applications
  • Employment history and skills information provided in applications
  • Cover notes and other application content

From employers:

  • Company name, registration number and business details
  • Contact person identity information (name, SA ID number, title)
  • Business verification documents (CIPC certificate, proof of address, ID)
  • Login credentials (email and encrypted password)
  • Billing and subscription information

Automatically collected:

  • Usage data and page views (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device type and browser information

3. How We Use Your Information

Job seeker information is used to:

  • Submit your application to the relevant employer
  • Allow employers to review and respond to your application
  • Send you job alert notifications if you have subscribed

Employer information is used to:

  • Verify your business identity before granting platform access
  • Manage your employer account and job listings
  • Process subscription invoices and payments
  • Display your company profile and listings to job seekers

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Job seeker applications are shared with the relevant employer only. We do not share applicant information with any other employer or third party.

Employer company profile information (company name, description, logo) is displayed publicly on the platform.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, hosted in the Africa South 1 (Johannesburg) region. Firebase provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

Access to employer verification documents is restricted to authorised Cronos Jobs personnel only.

6. Data Retention

Job applications are retained for 12 months after submission. Employer account data is retained for the duration of the account and for 3 years after closure for legal compliance purposes.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@cronos.co.za

7. Your Rights (POPIA)

In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

8. Cookies

Cronos Jobs uses Firebase Authentication which stores a session token in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Cronos Jobs is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. Continued use of the platform after changes are published constitutes acceptance.

Privacy enquiries
